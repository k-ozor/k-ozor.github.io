    About

    I am an Autistic American textile artist living in Berlin, Germany, with ex street cat Ali and blind rescue dog George. I make large scale embroidery pieces out of recycled leather and vegan leather, as well as digital drawings.

    I mostly create abstract landscapes and portraits. I'm inspired by late 1800s land survey photography, catholic imagery, old hollywood starlets, snowcapped mountains, and many other things.

    I accept commissions, and am open to selling and exhibiting my work. Just contact me via the form.
